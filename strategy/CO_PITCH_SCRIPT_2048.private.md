## INTRO CALL (30 min) — the script

### 1. Hook — ALEX (60 sec)
> "Tierva is a deep-tech Earth-intelligence platform — a system of record for what's true about any
> piece of land. The whole planet is now sensed for free at 10 m; the systems that *act* on that truth
> still wait on committees. We're the execution layer that closes that gap. First deployment: drought
> in Latin America, validated on real 2023 Honduras data."

### 2. Why now / why generational — ALEX (2 min)
Two curves crossed: (1) the planet is sensed continuously, free; (2) decisions still wait on humans.
The generational company turns raw EO into a per-parcel answer — *what's true → what it means → what to
do* — then settles outcomes automatically and auditably. Earth-intelligence is the category; ag land is
vertical #1, not the ceiling.

### 3. The ground truth — JUAN CARLOS (2–3 min) ← his block
> "I'm in Honduras. The Dry Corridor is ~20M people, mostly subsistence farmers. When drought hits,
> satellites see it in days — but relief takes months because it waits on sign-off. I do remote sensing
> at UNAH; I've seen this gap kill harvests. We validated our trigger on the real 2023 El Niño drought —
> it caught the real July event and *rejected* a false January signal. The data is real, the region is
> real, and I'm the one who can stand behind it on the ground."

### 4. The moat (lead here, NOT the satellites) — ALEX (2 min)
Open EO is a commodity input — never our moat. The moat **compounds with use**: ① fused proprietary
outcome/disbursement data, ② the per-parcel models (GAEZ + NEX-GDDP + 10 m embeddings), ③ the validator/
partner network, ④ auditable settlement rails. *That's* the system of record.

### 5. Traction — ALEX (90 sec) — keep honest per ⚠️ above
- Consensus integrity proven — ≥2 sensors must agree; **killed the January false-fire** (the failure
  mode that sinks naive parametric triggers).
- Real 2023 Honduras backtest, end-to-end, not a mock.
- L1 spine mostly built: 15 satellite loaders + map + multi-sensor panel.
- Contract built + 22 tests; **[public-testnet link if deployed].**
- IDB = a channel/wedge we're walking (say "channel," not "signed customer").

### 6. The ask + founder control — ALEX (90 sec)
> "We're raising on a post-money SAFE. Fast-Track range $250–750K fits where we are and keeps dilution
> low. The round's structured so I stay controlling CEO — first money in is a SAFE, no board seat, no
> control terms at this stage. We sell equity, not the company."

### 7. Q&A — both
Alex fields vision/tech/business/control. **Hand any Honduras/region/ground-validation question to JC.**
