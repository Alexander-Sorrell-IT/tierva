## Slide 1 — Title / one-liner
- **Tierva** — *the system of record for what's true about any piece of land.*
- Sub: *A deep-tech Earth-intelligence data platform with a compounding data moat — proven live on a public chain
  on real 2023 Honduras drought data.*
- Quiet credibility line: live contract • real-data backtest • IDB pilot in motion.

## Slide 2 — Why now (the two curves crossing)
- Curve 1: the whole planet is now sensed continuously, free, at 10 m → raw land-truth is a commodity.
- Curve 2: systems that *act* on it still wait on committees/adjusters/reports.
- **The gap is the company:** the intelligence + execution layer between sensing and acting.
- *(This is the "why generational" slide — their weakest-covered test. Land it hard.)*

## Slide 3 — The problem (commercial, not impact)
- Nobody owns the per-parcel answer. Free EO tools demand EO/Python literacy; "easy" agtech assumes you already
  farm a managed field. The un-served slot: *point at a parcel → get the truth, the meaning, the decision.*
- Today that answer is assembled by hand, slowly, by experts — and the *outcome* (pay/act) waits on humans.

## Slide 4 — The product: one system of record, three jobs
- **L1 SEE** (truth, now — mostly built) → **L2 UNDERSTAND** (meaning + forecast) → **L3 DECIDE / "gardener"**
  (what to do, 20-yr future-proofed — premium).
- One screen demo: a map pin → plain-language answer. Show, don't tell.

## Slide 5 — The moat (the make-or-break slide for 2048)
- ❌ NOT the satellite data (commodity). ✅ The moat **compounds with use:**
  1. fused EO + ground-truth + **outcome/disbursement data** (the system of record),
  2. the per-parcel **models** (GAEZ + NEX-GDDP + 10 m embeddings fused — no portal does this),
  3. the **validator/partner network** (network effects),
  4. the **auditable settlement rails** (defensible integration).
- Tagline: *every event makes the next answer better.*

## Slide 6 — Traction / proof (exceeds pre-seed median)
- Live, adversarially-verified instance on a public chain (clickable).
- Real 2023 Honduras backtest, end-to-end.
- Consensus integrity: ≥2 sensors must agree — **killed a January false-fire** (the failure mode that sinks naive
  triggers). This is the "we've already de-risked the hard part" slide.
- IDB pilot = wedge customer / distribution.

## Slide 7 — Business model
- Free spine = $0 data cost (free-for-commercial Copernicus/NASA; cost is compute, R2+TiTiler 20–300× cheaper).
- Premium where the free tier breaks: parcel close-up (Satellogic ~$4.50/km²), all-weather SAR.
- L3 "gardener" = premium decision layer funds the platform.

## Slide 8 — Market / wedge → expansion
- Vertical #1: agricultural land + drought (LAC, IDB wedge). One engine, many shocks (flood/frost/price), and
  beyond ag — any parametric decision that waits on a committee. *Vision expansive, ask is a wedge.*

## Slide 9 — Settlement rails (crypto, under the hood)
- "Auditable settlement rails": when the data confirms a condition, the outcome executes automatically and
  anyone can re-verify the exact inputs. **The customer watches the data that fires the payout.**
- Do NOT lead with chain/token. It's plumbing that makes the data trustworthy.
