## The three load-bearing reframes (or the deal dies on their core criterion)
1. **Moat ≠ the satellite data.** Open EO is a *commodity input* — it fails their proprietary-data test outright.
   The moat lives in: **fused EO + ground-truth + accumulated disbursement/payout-outcome data**, the **models**,
   and the **validator/partner network** — data that *compounds with use*. Land it there or the deal dies.
2. **Crypto goes under the hood** — call it *"automated, auditable settlement rails."* Don't lead with "live
   smart contract on testnet" to a fund with no current crypto thesis.
3. **IDB = wedge customer / distribution lock**, not a development-bank impact story. Strip the
   impact / dev-finance / "parametric insurance" vocabulary entirely — it reads off-strategy to a commercial VC.

**The one-liner to lead with:** *"A deep-tech Earth-intelligence **data platform / system of record** with a
compounding, defensible data moat — proven live on a public chain on real 2023 Honduras drought data."*
Pitch **only the Delaware C-corp**; keep the NM LLC infra-finance track out of the room.

## Two structural must-dos BEFORE pitching
1. **Round math — RESOLVED ✅.** The strategy doc's "$1–2M post-money SAFE" is the **amount raised** (it sits in
   a table of *amounts*: $150–750K grants → $1–2M seed), **not** a $1–2M valuation cap. So a 2048 $500K–$3M lead
   **fits cleanly and can anchor the whole round.** (If it had meant a $2M *cap*, the round would be mis-specified
   for them — but it doesn't.)
2. **Make Juan Carlos a TITLED CO-FOUNDER with equity.** Their #1 criterion is founder-market-fit, and they
   prefer complete teams over solo founders. **Nearly all the FMF rests on JC** (a US system-architect building
   Honduran drought finance is thin alone). If JC reads as a "teammate," we likely miss their bar. → *This is
   exactly what the 2026-06-04 Discord message to JC opened — asking him to be part of Tierva beyond the contest.*

## Sequencing (complementary to grants-first, NOT conflicting)
1. **Open the relationship NOW, pre-proof** — pre-traction is their lane, and a stretch-fit deal needs runway to
   build conviction. Waiting risks drifting into seed-stage where the bar + competition rise.
2. **Grants land in parallel** — VC-favorable (more proof, less dilution, higher cap), and grants are **not** a
   prior priced round → keeps us **Fast-Track-eligible** (<$2M).
3. **Proof-unlock = the TERM-SHEET trigger** (live on public chain + one real disbursement + IDB pilot signed) —
   the event that converts a warm conversation into a lead check at a stronger cap. *First contact now; the ask
   fires at the milestone.* Jun 11 is the hackathon pitch deadline, **not** a 2048 gate — use its demo as ammo.
