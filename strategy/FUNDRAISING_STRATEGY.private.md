## 1. THE GLOBAL IDEA (expanded)

**The category.** Pacto Seco is the first substrate for *self-executing parametric public finance on verifiable open data*: any climate or crisis trigger, any country, any payout — disbursed automatically the moment publicly recomputable satellite data confirms the event, with no human approval gap and no commercial oracle. The clean-sheet move is not "faster drought insurance." It is the deletion of the step that defines the incumbent: the human approval gap between detection (days) and relief (months). Satellites see the drought; committees, assessors, and ministerial sign-off delay the money. Pacto Seco removes the signer — the rule lives in the contract, the contract reads the sky, the cash moves itself. The precise framing: **not insurance — a self-executing line of a national budget.** Insurance is a product sold to a beneficiary; a self-executing budget line is infrastructure a state installs once and trusts forever.

**Why now.** Three curves crossed, and they only cross once:
- **Open satellite data went free and total.** Copernicus (Sentinel-2 greenness, ERA5 rainfall, Sentinel-1 radar) publishes the whole planet every few days at zero cost with permanent product IDs. The ground truth a parametric payout needs is now a public good — no commercial data deal required to start.
- **Programmable settlement went cheap and final.** A payout rule now lives as a few hundred lines of audited code executing itself for cents, no clearing bank, no discretionary intermediary. The "self-executing" half became buildable by a small team, not a consortium.
- **The shocks are accelerating** in the climate-vulnerable belt while relief machinery still runs on a months-long manual cadence. The gap between how fast disasters hit and how slowly money moves widens every year.

**The moat** is trust and category position, *not* proprietary tech — and we confront the copycat objection head-on:
- **Open recomputability is a trust property no incumbent can match.** Every payout is reconstructible by anyone from published free-Copernicus product IDs — a journalist, auditor, or opposition party can independently verify or falsify any reading that moved public money. Etherisc and Arbol are structurally locked to a commercial oracle (Chainlink): their trigger is a number you must *trust*, not a computation you can *redo*. For private insurance that's tolerable; for **public money**, auditability is the license to operate. A closed-oracle competitor cannot become open without rebuilding their core.
- **Multi-sensor consensus is the integrity layer, designed in.** NDVI greenness alone false-fired in January 2023 and would have blown the budget before the real El Niño drought arrived in July; requiring greenness *and* rainfall to agree removes that single point of failure, enforced on-chain.
- **The standard is the prize, not the patent.** Defensibility is the reference-implementation position — being the trusted substrate development banks and governments adopt and others build on. Copycats ratify the category and ideally build on our rails.

**The expansion path** — one substrate, scaled across magnitudes: (1) drought in the LAC Dry Corridor (proven first); (2) other triggers — flood, frost, heat, crop-price collapse, displacement — same consensus-and-disburse engine, different sensors; (3) other regions — Copernicus covers the planet, so the pattern replicates to the Sahel, South/SE Asia, any climate-exposed state; (4) a protocol others build on — the endpoint is a standard for self-executing public finance, with banks, ministries, and NGOs writing their own trigger rules onto shared, openly verifiable rails.

## 8. ENTITY & MECHANICS

**The binding constraint is STAGE, not legal structure.** Minimum-viable-structure now, layered later. Recommended spine:

1. **Win the hackathon / land the IDB pilot as an individual — form NOTHING first.** Per the contest terms the prize is a contract "to one individual on the team" (verify in rules). No entity is required to capture the first **~$50K (estimate — confirm against contest rules)**, or to receive Web3 public-goods grants (Gitcoin/Stellar/Celo) and Mercy Corps's equity-free check. Do not spend **$2K–$20K (estimate)** incorporating to win money you can receive personally.
2. **When the pilot is real and money/IP/liability needs a home: form ONE Delaware Public Benefit Corporation, taxed as a C-corp.** A PBC *is* a C-corp under the hood — standard NVCA docs, post-money SAFEs, **QSBS eligibility**, plus a charter that hard-wires the mission (PBCs have IPO'd: Lemonade, Warby Parker, Coursera) ([herzoglaw](https://herzoglaw.co.il/en/news-and-insights/white-paper-choosing-between-a-delaware-public-benefit-corporation-and-a-traditional-c-corporation-structure-for-generative-ai-start-ups/), [clerky](https://handbooks.clerky.com/legal-concepts/notes/public-benefit-corporations)). **This single entity serves both VC and IDB** — IDB Lab funds private companies directly ([bidlab](https://bidlab.org/en/products/financing-products)); you do NOT need a nonprofit. A nonprofit is required only for US tax-deductible *donations* / charitable foundation grants ([brombergerlaw](https://www.brombergerlaw.com/non-profit-for-profit-ventures)) — not on your roadmap, so don't build it.
3. **Raise pre-seed on a post-money SAFE** at a modest, stage-justified cap (SAFEs = 90% of pre-seed rounds, ~87% post-money — [carta](https://carta.com/learn/startups/fundraising/convertible-securities/pre-money-vs-post-money-safes/), [ycombinator](https://www.ycombinator.com/documents)). Keep "tested locally, not live" verbatim-honest in the deck — a low-but-honest cap is what an early believer expects, and overclaiming fails diligence. IDB grants and equity SAFEs coexist in the same PBC with no structural conflict.
4. **Defer the nonprofit / fiscal sponsorship** until a specifically *charitable* donor stream demands it. Do NOT build a dual C-corp + nonprofit "hybrid" now — that's a Series-A-era complication.

**Regulatory risk — the part to get exactly right.** The danger is *not* token issuance (have no token; keep it that way). The danger is **custodying/disbursing beneficiary funds**, which can make you a money transmitter / VASP.
- **US:** Don't rely on a "disbursing our own funds" exemption — FinCEN treats own-reserve exchange the same, and the GENIUS Act + April 2026 FinCEN/OFAC proposed rules pull stablecoin flows under the Bank Secrecy Act ([fincen](https://www.fincen.gov/news/news-releases/treasury-proposes-rule-implement-genius-acts-requirements-counter-illicit), [hklaw](https://www.hklaw.com/en/insights/publications/2026/04/fincen-and-ofac-propose-aml-sanctions-rules-for-stablecoin-issuers)).
- **Honduras — decisive:** CNBS **Resolution 003/2024 absolutely prohibits supervised financial institutions from holding/operating with crypto** ([cryptonews](https://cryptonews.com/news/honduras-tightens-crypto-regulations-prohibits-bank-transactions-and-holdings/), [decrypt](https://decrypt.co/218076/honduras-ban-crypto-bitcoin-versus-latin-central-america)). This **kills any "stablecoin straight into Honduran bank accounts" design.**
- **The architecture that shields you (and matches the framing):** Pacto Seco is the **trigger / consensus-oracle / accounting layer** — the contract decides *when* and *how much*, on-chain, auditably. The **actual fiat movement to households is done by a licensed local payer / mobile-money agent / government treasury.** Then Pacto Seco is not the custodian, sidesteps money-transmitter/VASP status, and stays clear of the CNBS ban (banks receive a fiat instruction, never touch crypto). This is exactly "a self-executing line of a national budget, not a wallet" — make it explicit in materials. Get a US fintech + Honduran counsel opinion letter before any *real-money* pilot (not before the demo).

*This is strategic guidance, not legal/tax advice — confirm the prize-to-individual term in the contest rules, and get counsel before any live-money pilot.*
